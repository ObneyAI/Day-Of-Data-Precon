What is AI, really?

An LLM is a very good next-word guesser.
The sky is     →blue
💡 It read a huge chunk of the internet and learned what usually comes next. It predicts text — it's a tool, not magic.

How does it "understand" you?

It doesn't read your words like we do — it turns them into a list of numbers (a vector) that captures meaning.

"waterproof jacket"→[ 0.12, -0.44, 0.98, ... ] 384 numbers
💡 Similar meanings → similar numbers. That's the secret behind everything we do today.
